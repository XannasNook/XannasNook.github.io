Attempting Fate

Attempting Fate is a vampire-themed visual novel written in C using a custom engine that utilized a DigiPen proprietary library called "DGL". This game was developed for DigiPen's GAM-150 class. The story follows the player, taking the role of either Aleksandr, Karda, or Amaroth, through their attempt to overthrow the king, who is opressing vampire-kind. The player gains items which effect their stats, which change the dialogue options that can be chosen.

I worked as a gameplay and engine programmer for this project. My biggest contribution being the custom scripting language, which allowed our narrative writer the ability to change backgrounds, add dialogue options, go to and return from, play animations, and change the currently displayed character.
