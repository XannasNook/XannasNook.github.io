Fish Fear Me

Fish Fear Me is an absurdist, open-world, fishing creature-collector made in C++ with OpenGL and FMOD and is a school project. In Fish Fear Me, you roam an open world where you catch fish through a rhythm-like fishing mechanic. Which fish you get is calculated by a dynamically updating system, with fish "hotspots", and as you complete regions, you unlock new areas with new types of fish.

I worked as the Tech Lead on the custom engine for Fish Fear Me. I created the underlying ECS structure, which stores entities as indices that can be used to find components in a data hashmap.

This project is currently ongoing, and is set to release Q2 of 2026.
