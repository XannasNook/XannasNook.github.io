About Me

Hi! I'm Xanna. A Seattle-based programmer. I have been interested in being a developer since I could talk. I taught myself Unity at 9 and have been making games ever since. I started working as a programmer for an online vrchat community called "Automated Souls". After years of hard work I got into DigiPen, my dream school. At digipen I have learned many industry standards and have strengthened myself as a gameplay programmer.

I have worked on many projects that you can see on my portfolio. Before going to DigiPen, I participated as the programmer in the month-long GCPL game jam with a designer friend, where we made the game "As You Stare". That game jam taught me an immense amount, as it was my first finished and published game. Since then, I have worked on 3 game projects at DigiPen: "Quality Computer Battles" as a gameplay programmer, "Attempting Fate" as an engine and scripting programmer, and I am currently working on "Fish Fear Me" as Technical Lead.
